Starting Over + An Introduction

Hello to all that read this! I thought that now's as good a time as any to start over and update my "blog". First things first, my name is Preston, aka vaugerbird, the owner of this page. I'm currently a college student at Oregon State University studying electrical and computer engineering, and I'm currently the president of the Amateur Radio Club at OSU (W7OSU).

I am an avid enthusiast of anything electronic and amateur radio related, as evidenced by my project portfolio. Speaking of which, please feel free to check out my Projects page if you haven't already! I will be updating the page when I get some free time with pictures and links, as well as some cool things I've built in the past.

Also, if you are here because of my sstv-backpack project, welcome! This is the first project that I've ever published to get any traction, so thank you for your support!
